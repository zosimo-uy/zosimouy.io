My quick story

My journey into the world of technology began with a childhood fascination for computer games. This early interest ignited my curiosity and propelled me into exploring the intricate workings of computer hardware and technology.

Driven by this passion, I pursued a B.S. in Computer Engineering, where I immersed myself in the fields of electronics, mechatronics, and the practical application of digital systems. My hands-on experience with microcontrollers further solidified my fascination with the convergence of electronics and programming.

Now equipped with a robust technical background and a hunger for continuous learning, I am eager to apply my expertise to assist clients with their technology-related challenges. I am committed to delivering exceptional technical support and am enthusiastic about the opportunity to contribute my skills to a team where I can make a meaningful impact.
