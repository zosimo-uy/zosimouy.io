My quick story

I delved into the world of computers at the age of 10, initially captivated by games. Through gaming, I gained hands-on experience with computer hardware, which sparked my curiosity and set the foundation for my computer journey. As I grew more familiar with technology, I began customizing my Friendster profile, adding URL links, editing text and backgrounds, and even incorporating music. These early experiences allowed me to explore my creative side and express myself through digital mediums.

Driven by my passion, I pursued a B.S. in Computer Engineering, as it seemed a natural progression for someone whose life had been profoundly impacted by computers. Throughout my studies, I immersed myself in electronics, mechatronics, and the practical application of digital I/O and sensors. Although my programming exposure was limited, I used microcontrollers to automate processes and develop industrial machines, further cementing my passion for the intersection of electronics and programming.

In recent months, I made a pivotal decision to switch careers and enrolled in a full-stack web development bootcamp. Through determination and hard work, I successfully passed their technical exam and completed the required activities, projects, and capstone to earn certification under Kodego PH.

This journey into web development reignited my love for programming and allowed me to rediscover my true calling. Alongside my team members, I honed my communication skills, offering assistance and support whenever they encountered challenges. In turn, their struggles became valuable learning opportunities for me, fostering personal growth and development.

As a junior developer

I always prioritize the readability of my code. It serves as a valuable tool for reviewing and updating my code when necessary. Moreover, it enables others to easily understand and reuse my code. By making my code highly readable, I aim to create a sense of freedom, allowing anyone to make edits and modifications as needed.

I strive to maintain a codebase that is clean, consistent, and easy to follow. A clean codebase not only brings a sense of relief when everything works smoothly, but it also ensures that the code remains unchanged regardless of who interacts with it. I believe in the power of maintainability, allowing for seamless collaboration and future development.

I'm thrilled to embark on new projects because each one presents an opportunity for personal and professional growth. With every project, I learn something new and expand my skill set. This excitement fuels my passion for development, as I embrace challenges and eagerly explore innovative solutions. The joy of continuous learning and the satisfaction of overcoming obstacles drive me forward in my journey as a developer.

As a person

If I had to describe myself in one word, it would be resourceful. I encounter problems with a determined mindset, breaking them down into smaller, manageable pieces. Through my improvisation and creative thinking, I find unique solutions that help me navigate even the toughest obstacles.

Curiosity drives me, and I thrive on continuous learning and exploration. I have absorbed a wide range of skills and developed diverse interests over time. When these elements are brought together, they nourish my career and personal growth, enabling me to adapt and excel in various endeavors.

As a colleague

I bring with me a wealth of experience from my previous role in a BPO company, specifically as a customer care technical support representative. This experience has shaped me into a dedicated team player and mentor, always ready to share my knowledge and support others by leveraging the expertise I have gained through years of hands-on experience. Even while attending to my own customers, I made sure to be accessible and approachable to my colleagues, ensuring that I remained available to address their queries and provide assistance.

My time in customer care technical support has significantly enhanced my communication skills. Dealing with a diverse range of customer inquiries, I have become adept at conveying ideas and resolving issues in various ways, adapting my communication style to cater to both technical and non-technical individuals. I have learned to communicate complex concepts in a manner that is easily understandable, enabling effective collaboration and problem-solving.

Throughout my career journey, from my initial role to my most recent position, I have been exposed to a ladderized approach in the tech industry. This progression has allowed me to gain valuable insights into the inner workings of a tech company while consistently delivering industry-standard results on the production floor. Notably, I have acquired extensive knowledge and experience with Apple products, including their mobile devices, tablets, accessories, and Mac computers.
